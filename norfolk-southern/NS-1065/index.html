📖 History

Norfolk Southern 1065 honors the Savannah & Atlanta Railway as part of Norfolk Southern's Heritage Unit program. The locomotive was painted in the railroad's historic colors to recognize its contribution to railroading in the southeastern United States while preserving its legacy for future generations.

Built as a GE ET44AC, NS 1065 remains in active freight service while serving as a moving tribute to the Savannah & Atlanta Railway. Since entering service in its heritage paint scheme, the locomotive has become a sought-after unit among railfans across the Norfolk Southern system.

📖 History

Norfolk Southern 1065 honors the Savannah & Atlanta Railway as part of Norfolk Southern's Heritage Unit program.

Built as a GE ET44AC, the locomotive remains active in freight service while preserving the legacy of the Savannah & Atlanta Railway.

🏛 Welcome

Welcome to the TrainsForever Museum Archive! This museum was created to preserve railroad history through photographs, videos, and documented encounters collected by TrainsForever since 2017. As locomotives are repainted, retired, rebuilt, or disappear from service, this archive provides a permanent place where future generations can continue to enjoy these remarkable pieces of railroad history.

Whether you're here to explore heritage locomotives, discover rare railroad equipment, or simply enjoy the history behind each exhibit, welcome to the museum.

🧭 Museum Guide

How to Explore the Museum

The TrainsForever Museum Archive is organized by railroad. Choose a railroad collection from the Museum Lobby to begin exploring.

Inside each railroad collection you'll find categories such as:

  • 🚂 Heritage Units — Heritage locomotives honoring predecessor railroads.
  • ⭐ Special Interest — Anniversary units, first responder units, commemorative locomotives, and other notable equipment.
  • 📸 Rarities — Rare catches, uncommon consists, and unique railroad moments documented by TrainsForever.

Select any locomotive or exhibit to view photographs, videos, archive statistics, historical information, and curator's notes.

ℹ️ About the Museum

Why This Museum Exists

The TrainsForever Museum Archive was created to preserve railroad history through photographs, videos, historical research, and personal documentation. Every exhibit is designed to preserve locomotives and railroad equipment for future generations to enjoy long after they have been repainted, retired, or rebuilt.

📰 Curator's Desk

A Note From the Curator

Every exhibit in this museum is built using photographs, videos, historical research, and personal encounters collected by TrainsForever since 2017. The goal is simple: preserve railroad history accurately and make it easy for everyone to explore.

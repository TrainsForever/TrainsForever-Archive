🏛️ Welcome

Welcome to the TrainsForever Museum Archive. This exhibit preserves Norfolk Southern SD70ACe No. 1065 through photographs, historical information, documentation, and museum records. Every exhibit is designed to permanently archive notable locomotives for future railfans.

📖 Railroad History

Norfolk Southern 1065 is an SD70ACe diesel-electric locomotive built by Electro-Motive Diesel (EMD). As part of Norfolk Southern's modern fleet, the locomotive has operated across much of the eastern United States, hauling intermodal, manifest, and mixed freight service.

This exhibit documents one of TrainsForever's preserved encounters with the locomotive and permanently archives its place within the museum collection.

👋 Welcome

Welcome to the Savannah & Atlanta Heritage Exhibit.

Norfolk Southern Heritage Unit NS 1065 honors the legacy of the historic Savannah & Atlanta Railroad. This exhibit preserves the history of the railroad while documenting my own encounters with NS 1065 through photographs, videos, and personal records.

Within this exhibit you'll find railroad history, locomotive information, museum statistics, featured media, and my documented catches of this Heritage Unit.
